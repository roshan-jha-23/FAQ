Frequently Asked Questions

How do i Know my Shoe size?
With the outline, measure a straight line from your heel to the longest toe. This gives you the length of your foot in centimeters.
how to return my order?
Usually, you can drop off returns at the seller’s shipping carrier location for a full refund of the items you ordered. Any delivery & service fees you paid aren’t refunded. The shipping label is paid for.
Where will my refund hit?
Allow up to 10 business days for your refund to appear on your card or bank statement. The 10–day period starts when you get the email about the refund being issued. Remember that shipping times for returns to international return warehouses may be longer..
